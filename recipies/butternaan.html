Butter Naan

ingredients in Butter Naan :

  • Mailda
  • yogurt
  • Butter
  • Baking powder
  • sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Yeast

Steps to make Butter naan :

  1. Mix flour, yogurt, melted butter, baking powder, sugar, and salt to form a dough.
  2. Add warm water as needed to make a soft, smooth dough.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rest for 2 hours to ferment.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them into rounds.
  5. Heat a griddle or tava on medium-high heat.
  6. Cook the naan until it bubbles and puffs up on one side
  7. Flip and cook until golden brown with browned spots on the other side.
  8. Remove from heat and brush with more melted butter or ghee.
  9. Repeat for remaining dough balls.
  10. Serve your delicious butter naan warm with your favorite curries or dips.
